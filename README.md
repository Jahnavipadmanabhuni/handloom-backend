Handloom Direct – Full Stack E-Commerce Platform

Handloom Direct is a full-stack web application designed to connect customers directly with handloom workers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair trade. The platform empowers artisans by giving them direct access to the market while offering customers authentic, handmade products at reasonable prices.

This project aims to promote traditional handloom industries and support local artisans by providing a digital marketplace. Customers can browse, search, and purchase handloom products such as sarees, fabrics, and handicrafts directly from the creators.

The system is built with a modern full-stack architecture, ensuring scalability, performance, and ease of use. It includes both frontend and backend components integrated through REST APIs.
